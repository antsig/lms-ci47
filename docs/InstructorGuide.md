# Instructor Guide

This guide is designed to help instructors manage courses, students, and content effectively on the LMS platform.

## Dashboard

The instructor dashboard provides a snapshot of your teaching activities, including course performance and revenue.

## Course Management

### Creating a Course

1.  Navigate to **Course Manager** > **Create Course**.
2.  Fill in the course details (Title, Description, Category, Level, Price, etc.).
3.  Upload a thumbnail and configure settings (Free/Paid).

### Editing a Course

1.  Go to **My Courses**.
2.  Click the **Edit** button on the course you wish to modify.
3.  Update course details, settings, or curriculum.

### Managing Curriculum

You can structure your course using Sections and Lessons.

- **Add Section**: Organize content into modules or chapters.
- **Add Lesson**: Add content to sections. Supported types include:
  - **Video**: YouTube, Vimeo, or HTML5 (MP4).
  - **Text/Document**: Written content or attachments.

### Assessments

- **Quizzes**: Create quizzes with time limits and questions (Multiple choice).
- **Assignments**: Create assignments with descriptions and deadlines for students to submit work.

## Student Management

- **Enrolled Students**: View a list of students enrolled in your courses.
- **Progress**: (If available) Track student progress through the course material.

## Financials

- **Revenue Report**: View your earnings from paid courses.
