# User Guide (Student)

Welcome to the Learning Management System (LMS). This guide will help you navigate the platform as a student.

## getting Started

1.  **Login**: Access the platform using your credentials.
2.  **Dashboard**: Upon logging in, you will be taken to your dashboard where you can see an overview of your progress.

## Features

### Course Management

- **My Courses**: View all the courses you are currently enrolled in.
- **Wishlist**: Save courses you are interested in for later. You can add or remove courses from your wishlist at any time.

### Learning Interface (Course Player)

When you start a course, you will enter the Course Player.

- **Navigation**: Use the sidebar to navigate between sections and lessons.
- **Content**: View video lectures or read text materials.
- **Progress**: Your progress is tracked automatically as you complete lessons.

### Assessments

- **Quizzes**: Take quizzes to test your knowledge.
- **Assignments**: detailed assignments required by your instructors.

### Profile Settings

- **Edit Profile**: Update your personal information.
- **Change Password**: Secure your account by updating your password regularly.
